Result driven physical therapy.

Ready to transform your life with MEND?

MEND Physical therapy gives true one-on-one care. At MEND we don’t use aides or assistants. You will only work with an expert physical therapist for the entire session, resulting in fewer visits and better results.

At MEND we treat your injury and your entire body. We believe while an injury may be in one body part, true success comes from treating the body as a whole.

MEND puts you in the driver’s seat. Because MEND offers cash-pay, expert physical therapy, it allows you to select your treatment options a la cart. You and your therapist will determine your best treatment option for your long-term recovery needs.

Services

Just a few of the things we can do to help you.

Physical Therapy

We will talk layout the history of your pain and what you can’t do because of your pain. We will look at how you move, how much you are moving (your range of motion) and your strength. We will then come up with a plan.

Pilates

We teach whole body strengthening to improve both mobility and body mechanics to reduce further injury. If you are looking for just Pilates training with a Physical Therapist, those sessions are scheduled through MOVE.

Home Visits

When coming to our office is not possible due to the your mobility, we'll make arrangements to come to your home. We can provide expert manual therapy including joint mobilization, soft tissue work, or fascial release.
